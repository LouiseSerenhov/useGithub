Use Github, step by step

An easy way to save your code, use version control and work in a team!
  • Step 1:

    - Login to your Github account and press “repositories”. Find the one you forked from Louise, "portfolio template".

  • Step 2:

    - Press the green button in the right corner “Clone or download” and copy that url.

  • Step 3:

    - Go to your terminal and find your way to the desktop by writing:

    cd Desktop

    - When you are in the desktop at the terminal you can write the following in the terminal:

    git clone “the url you just copied”

  • Step 4:

    - Now you have the repository you created on your computor.

    - Start create your portfolio!
